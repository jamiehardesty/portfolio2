An introduction:

Who am I?

I am an LGBTQ+ identifying cis-female of 2 or more races. My pronouns are she/her. In 2010, I left the city of Chicago for Silicon Valley. Currently, I'm in Engineering Immersion at Thinkful. Also, I'm blissfully unaware of what makes me special. Maybe my contribution to diversity metrics?

My passion for the web:

It started in my childhood. I was treated as a neutral entity by most netizens, and that made me feel accepted for the first time.

How do I define a great team or programmer?

In my eyes, anyone who actively self-teaches themselves new things to positively influence the world is great. Therefore, a great team, in my opinion, improves the world or internet culture. Thankfully, there is plenty of trust & safety, escalation, anti-evil, and anti-fraud teams.

My personality outside of coding:

I hike. I visit rose gardens. I prefer cats over dogs. I have over 15 types of tea. My favorite colors are pink, red, and gold. I have a very distinct lewk. I try to be plant-based.

Services I can provide:

I am adept at HTML5 and CSS3. Intermediate with JS ES2015 or ES6. I write my tests in npm nyc, mocha, chai. I can make web requests, utilize axios, create promises, and write asynchronous code. I also have a working knowledge of LESS.css, Bootstrap, and React. I aspire to add go lang, Java, and SQL to my skillset.

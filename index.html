An introduction:

Who am I?

Hello! I'm Jamie. I live in Silicon Valley. I'm in Engineering Immersion at Thinkful. If you asked me, "what makes me special?" I'd joke, "my contribution to diversity metrics. An LGBT female of 2 or more races."

My passion for the web:

One of my grandparents suggested to my parents that I shouldn't learn their native tongue. This created an awkward language & cultural barrier at home that I never experienced online. I felt accepted for the first time!

How do I  define a great team or programmer?

In my eyes, anyone who actively self-teaches themselves new skills to positively influence the world is great. Therefore, a great team positively impacts the world or internet culture. Thankfully, there is plenty of trust & safety, escalation, anti-evil, and anti-fraud teams.

My personality outside of coding:

I hike. I visit rose gardens. I prefer cats over dogs. I have over 15 types of tea. My favorite colors are pink, red, and gold.

Services I can provide:

I am adept at HTML5 and CSS3. Intermediate with JS ES2015 or, colloquially, ES6. I write my tests in npm nyc, mocha, chai. I make web requests, utilize axios, create promises, and write asynchronous code. I have a working knowledge of LESS.css, Bootstrap, and React. I aspire to add go lang, Java, and SQL to my skill set.
